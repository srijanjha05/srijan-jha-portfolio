Life is More Than Tech

While I love building and innovating with technology, I believe the most impactful solutions come from a place of broad understanding and diverse experience. My passions outside of code—from policy debate to the performing arts—give me a unique perspective on problem-solving and a deeper appreciation for the human side of innovation.

Model United Nations

An active debater and policy thinker, I have represented institutions at both national and international MUN platforms. At Harvard Model UN India 2021, I earned the Outstanding Delegate Award (2nd out of 85 delegates). At PES MUN Society, I currently serve as Finance Lead, managing the budgeting for a 500+ delegate national conference. I have also won Best Delegate among 40 participants at UniCon 2025, securing ₹10,000 in prize money.

TeleMedi

Co-founded a lockdown-era healthcare outreach initiative that connected 100+ hospitals to the Telemedicine Society of India. I worked directly with medical professionals to communicate government guidelines, safety protocols, and telemedicine best practices, ensuring safe and effective patient care in a time of urgent need.

C.H.A.N.G.E. – Sustainable Development Policy

As part of Sanskriti School’s Student Council, I led an environmental awareness survey for 100+ students, identifying key gaps in sustainability understanding. I co-designed the C.H.A.N.G.E. framework—a comprehensive green policy proposal—to help the school adopt eco-friendly practices and promote environmental responsibility.

Theatre & Performing Arts

Selected for The National School of Drama’s prestigious student program, I trained intensively in stagecraft, expression, and body movement. I was entrusted with Assistant Director responsibilities for the play “The Lion, The Witch, and The Wardrobe”, guiding 10+ actors in emotional delivery and gesture work to elevate the production’s performance quality.
